How It Works

A fully automated pipeline from raw image to Gold Standard training data

📤

1. Upload Images

AI companies bulk-upload raw medical images. Our platform auto-detects department (X-ray → Radiology, etc.) and anonymizes all patient data.

🎯

2. Smart Assignment

Images are pushed to verified specialists matching the department — Radiologists for X-rays, Dermatologists for skin scans, and so on.

✏️

3. Expert Annotation

Doctors use professional canvas tools to circle tumors, mark fractures, highlight anomalies, and add clinical labels.

🔍

4. Peer QA Review

A second verified specialist reviews each annotation. Only 100% approved annotations move forward.

💰

5. Doctor Gets Paid

Upon approval, the annotating doctor earns $4 per image (configurable). Transparent, per-image compensation.

📦

6. Gold Standard Data

AI companies download annotated datasets in COCO JSON, Pascal VOC, or YOLO format — ready to train models.
